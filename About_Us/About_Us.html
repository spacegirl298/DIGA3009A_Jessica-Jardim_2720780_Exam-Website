ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Safe Dollar Studio was founded in July 2024 in Johannesburg, South Africa, by a group of five second-year students who first collaborated at a campus game jam, earning an honourable mention. This recognition led to a mentorship with TwoNameGames, which guided them in establishing the studio as a formal indie game company. By March 2025, the team had grown to six members, and in July 2025, they achieved further recognition by winning Best Game at another campus game jam. Currently, Safe Dollar Studio is focused on its “Safe Dollar Season,” producing a new game prototype every two weeks, with a commitment to delivering playful, innovative experiences to the gaming community.

OUR MISSION & VALUES

Our mission is to create playful, engaging games that spark joy and curiosity, bringing imaginative experiences to players of all ages.

  • Collaboration: We work as a united team, supporting each other to produce quality work.
  • Adaptability: We embrace feedback and guidance with openness, always ready to refine our ideas or let go of what doesn’t serve the project.
  • Ambition: We are driven to grow within the industry, striving to improve with each game we create.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated not only to our projects but also to the broader creative community, viewing our journey as part of something larger than ourselves.
