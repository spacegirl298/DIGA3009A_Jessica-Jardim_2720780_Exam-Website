SKY ISLES

Collect gems and coins in this charming 3D platformer adventure! Run, jump, and dash through light platforming challenges, discovering secrets and hidden paths along the way. Solve clever, yet approachable puzzles to reach your objectives and make your way through each vibrant level. Use the coins you collect to unlock fun items, including hearts, gems, and a variety of hats to customize your character. Explore three intricately designed levels and a lively hub world, each filled with opportunities for exploration and discovery. Can you find every gem and complete the game? Challenge yourself to uncover all the treasures and master each level!
