ROGUE SLASHER

Destroy waves of enemies, collect rare drops, and craft powerful gear! Rogue Slasher is an addictive third-person shooter that challenges you to survive as long as possible against relentless waves of enemies. Hunt, fight, and upgrade yourself to stay ahead of increasingly dangerous foes. But survival won’t be easy—enemies grow stronger with each wave, and formidable bosses appear to test your skills and strategy. Collect rare item drops, craft powerful gear, and adapt your playstyle to overcome escalating challenges. Do you have what it takes to slash your way through endless chaos and rise to the top?
