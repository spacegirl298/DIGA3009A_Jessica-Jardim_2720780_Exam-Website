THREE MAN TEAM

Work with three unique characters in this puzzle-platformer adventure! The Green character is quick on his feet and can leap to impressive heights, helping you reach distant platforms and dodge tricky obstacles. The Red character may move slowly and jump lower, but his strength allows him to push heavy red boxes—perfect for creating new paths or activating switches. The Blue character has access to teleporters, letting you instantly shift across the level and navigate complex layouts with ease. Master each character’s abilities and cleverly combine their strengths to overcome challenges, solve platforming puzzles, and reach the end of every level!
