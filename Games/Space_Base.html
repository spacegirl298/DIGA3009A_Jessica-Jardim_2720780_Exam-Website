SPACE BASE

Survive as long as you can in this fast–paced action tower-defense prototype! The tower at the center of the map is your lifeline—defend it at all costs. Spherical enemies will swarm from every direction, growing stronger and more numerous with each wave. Fight them off by shooting enemies yourself, earning money with every takedown. Use your hard-earned resources to place turrets, support structures, and strategic defenses that help you hold the line. As waves escalate, quick thinking and smart placement become essential. Last as long as possible and see how far your defenses can truly go!
