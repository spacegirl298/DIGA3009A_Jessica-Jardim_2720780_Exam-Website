About the Game

A side-scrolling shoot 'em up where a rebel crew battles powerful corporations. Dodge attacks, defeat enemy waves, and unleash devastating ultimates in a fast-paced, retro-inspired adventure.

Features

  • Classic side-scrolling shoot 'em up action
  • Multiple ship types with unique abilities
  • Devastating ultimate attacks

Gameplay

Take control of a rebel spacecraft and battle against corporate forces in intense aerial combat. Collect power-ups, upgrade your weapons, and survive increasingly difficult waves of enemies across desert landscapes.
