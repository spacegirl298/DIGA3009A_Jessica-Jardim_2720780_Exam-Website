COZYCORNERS

Step into the mess and make magic in CozyCorners—an isometric cleaning and decorating game where you turn chaotic homes into warm, inviting, and beautifully personalized spaces. Tackle cluttered rooms at your own pace as you sweep, scrub, vacuum, and tidy up every corner until everything feels just right. Once the space is spotless, unleash your creativity by choosing furniture, colors, and décor that reflect your style. From picking the perfect wallpaper to arranging cozy details, every action is crafted to feel deeply satisfying, calming, and visually rewarding.
