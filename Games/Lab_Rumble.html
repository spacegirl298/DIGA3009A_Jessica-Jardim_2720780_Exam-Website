LAB RUMBLE

Lab Rumble is a fast-paced 2D projectile-based brawler set in the final days of a collapsing Earth. Two brilliant scientists, racing against time, have been working tirelessly to cure a devastating global plague known as Exploding Head Syndrome. But with humanity on the brink and only one escape ship left, their partnership shatters. In a chaotic, cluttered laboratory, players must grab anything they can—beakers, gadgets, machinery parts, unstable experiments—and launch them at their opponent using wild improvised weapons. Every object becomes a projectile, every corner a trap, and every second a fight for survival. Only one scientist can make it off the dying planet. Outsmart, out-throw, and outlast your rival. In Lab Rumble, the last survivor wins.
